Smart Home Assistant

Nowadays, AI devices are making us more advance and making our life easier. By automation, these devices work to simplify our work and make our time more valuable. The smart mirror is one of them. We can use a smart mirror to view the reflection of us and as a smart device. For example, we can use it for music player, video player, task reminder, and so much more. There are some smart mirrors in the market, but all of these features are static where users need to give commands to execute operations. So, we will build an advanced smart mirror that will work with AI. Peoples forgot to turn off the light, fans, water pump, etc. But smart home assistant is only one smart device that will detect users at home with object deject and movement by their cameras and identify that whether users are in-house or not. According to detection, it will set the commands automatically. It also uses for home security, smart home to turn on or off other devices when needed, and we can use it through the android application from anywhere. The smart mirror will authenticate real users by their password, face, and voice.
